About Sign Hub

Only Cool Sign & Print - Signs that build business

Despite minimal online presence and promotion, Sign Hub has earned a strong reputation in the signage industry. Our meticulous craftsmanship and dedication to quality have fostered positive word-of-mouth referrals from satisfied clients. Specializing in digital printing and signage, we offer a wide range of products, including yard signs, custom signage, business signs, graphic banners, outdoor signage, and promotional sale signs. At Sign Hub, we bring our clients' visions to life with fast turnaround times and cost-effective solutions.

Who We Are

Sign Hub is a proudly Kiwi-owned business that started as a small, home-based venture in 2010. In 2014, we transitioned to digital printing and have since collaborated with a diverse range of clients, from small businesses to large enterprises. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has positioned us as a trusted name in the signage industry.

Our Mission

At Sign Hub, we are committed to managing and fulfilling all of our clients' signage needs. From concept to completion, we serve as a trusted project partner, ensuring our clients' visions are brought to life with precision and excellence.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the premier choice for interior and exterior signage, as well as vehicle graphics, tailored to fit any budget. We achieve this by providing innovative business solutions that deliver real value to our clients. Integrity, professionalism, and customer-centric service are the cornerstones of our operations, ensuring a seamless and satisfying experience for every client.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What services do you offer?

We provide graphic design, custom printing, branding materials, and promotional products. Whether you need business cards, banners, posters, or merchandise printing, we’ve got you covered!

2. Do you offer custom designs?

Yes! Our graphic designers can create unique and personalized designs based on your needs. You can also provide your own artwork, and we’ll ensure it’s print-ready.

3. What file formats do you accept for printing?

We accept high-resolution files in PDF, AI, PSD, PNG, and JPEG formats. If you’re unsure, send us your file, and we’ll check if it meets print requirements.

4. How can I place an order?

You can place an order through our website, by phone, or by visiting our store. Simply choose your product, upload your design (if applicable), and confirm the details.

5. Do you offer bulk printing discounts?

Absolutely! We offer special pricing for bulk orders. Contact us for a custom quote based on your quantity and specifications.
